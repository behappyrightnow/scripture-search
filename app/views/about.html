Scripture Search

Wouldn't it be wonderful we had easy access to the timeless scriptures of humanity? A lot of confusion and misunderstanding is spread by not having direct access to a culture's deepest scriptures. The vision behind Scripture Search is to bring the benefits of the information technology revolution to ancient scriptures.

Just imagine, someday you could type the phrase "non-violence" and directly find what major world scriptures have to say on that topic.
